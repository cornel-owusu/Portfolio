Problem

M-KOPA had 120,000 monthly visits and 3,500 keywords but ranked at an average position 18. Low organic visibility in competitive fintech and solar markets across Kenya, Uganda, Nigeria & Ghana.

Strategy

Identified high-intent keywords (pay-as-you-go solar, affordable solar systems). Applied technical SEO fixes — slow loading speed, missing meta descriptions, duplicate title tags, broken links. Built SEO blog content and off-page strategy with renewable energy blogs & fintech media.

Results

Exceeded all KPI targets set at campaign launch.
